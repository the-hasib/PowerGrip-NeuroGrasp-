## Currently Under Development

---

## The Vision
Imagine losing the ability to hold a cup of coffee. For millions of stroke survivors, this is reality.
**PowerGrip (NeuroGrasp)** is our attempt to bridge the gap between expensive medical equipment and affordable home therapy. We are building a smart exoskeleton that "listens" to muscle signals and helps patients regain hand mobility.

## Why This Matters
Recovery shouldn't be boring or inaccessible. We are working on:
* **Mind-Reading Control:** Using **EMG sensors** to detect intent and assist movement.
* **Mirror Therapy:** A mode where the robotic hand mimics the healthy hand (Neuroplasticity).
* **Smart Safety:** An "Artificial Reflex" system that prevents crushing delicate objects.
* **Remote Doctor Access:** IoT integration so doctors can monitor progress from anywhere.
