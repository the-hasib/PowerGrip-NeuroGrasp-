## ğŸ‘‹ The Vision
Imagine losing the ability to hold a cup of coffee. For millions of stroke survivors, this is reality.
**PowerGrip (NeuroGrasp)** is our attempt to bridge the gap between expensive medical equipment and affordable home therapy. We are building a smart exoskeleton that "listens" to muscle signals and helps patients regain hand mobility.
